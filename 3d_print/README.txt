# Summary

These are the mechanical parts for building up an environment monitoring station, consisting of a Nova SDS011 dust sensor and a Bosch BME680 breakout board for temperature, humidity, pressure and gas concentration. There is also some space for a custom board which may be equipped with a voltage regulator and an ESP 8266 WiFi chip as in my device. The vents on bottom side are covered by small pieces of fly screen, taped into the two small frames.
